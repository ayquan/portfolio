January 2025 Northeast Los Angeles Wildfires

HTML, 2025.

This website was made during the January 2025 California wildfires. I made the website as a way to inform friends and family about the day-to-day status of the Eaton fire, how much northeast LA areas were being affected, and what areas should evacuate. In particular, this website was a helpful tool for my grandparents who were less aware of the situation.

Some key features of the website included a map of northeast Los Angeles, a button that listed evacuation zones, information on air quality, and a containment bar showing the percentage of fire being contained.
